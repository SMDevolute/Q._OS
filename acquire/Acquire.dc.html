Path A
Lead-led priced round
  • Lead · private, €6M sets price
  • Co-investors · 1–3 to fill to {{ roundLabel }}
  • Strategic / public · optional €2M co-invest
Cleanest structure: a lead prices the round and co-investors fill behind them. Everything hinges on landing the lead.
Switch trigger
Path B
Strategic-anchored
  • Strategic / public · €2–4M in early de-risks
  • Lead · private, €4–5M
  • Co-investors · fill the balance
Bringing a strategic or public co-investor in early signals validation and de-risks the raise, but a lead is still required to price and carry governance.
Switch trigger
Path C
Hybrid · debt / convertible top-up
  • Equity lead · smaller ticket, €3–4M
  • Co-investors · fill part of the round
  • Debt / convertible · bridge tranche to close
Fallback if equity appetite stalls: venture debt or a convertible carries the gap to close and converts into the next round. Watch cost of capital and conversion terms.
Switch trigger
